Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect facility details including name, address, and type during registration. Administrator contact information like name, email, and phone number is required. Patient data entered into the system is stored securely. Usage data helps us improve platform performance and features.

2. How We Use Information

Your information enables us to provide and maintain our services. Facility data helps identify your institution within the healthcare network. Contact information allows for important communications and support. Aggregated anonymized data may be used for health system analysis and reporting.

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Access to sensitive information is restricted to authorized personnel only. Regular security audits help maintain system integrity. All data transmissions are encrypted using secure protocols.

4. Data Sharing

We may share data with government health authorities as required by law. Partner organizations assisting with service delivery may access necessary data. No patient data will be shared for commercial purposes. You will be notified of any significant changes to data sharing practices.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to your facility's stored information at any time. Corrections to inaccurate data can be submitted through your account. Account deletion requests will be processed within legal requirements. Certain data must be retained to comply with healthcare regulations.

6. Policy Changes

We may update this policy to reflect changes in laws or services. Significant changes will be communicated through your registered email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. The effective date at the top indicates when changes were implemented.
